The link cruncher recognizes anything between two of the following character as a link:

Top level domain

Include http/https, www and the last slash. Example: https://www.bi.no/

Domain first directories

Enter all first directories of the domain, separated by commas (including slashes). Example: to enter bi.no/studier-og-kurs/ and bi.no/studere-ved-bi/, enter "/studier-og-kurs/, /studere-ved-bi/".
